About

Overview

Are you dreaming of a getaway where nature takes center stage? The Cottage Package at River Tern Lodge in Bhadra is your perfect escape. Nestled on a hillock, these cozy cottages come with private verandas offering breathtaking views of the Bhadra Reservoir. It's like having a front-row seat to nature's finest show. With hearty buffet meals served in the lodge's charming Gol Ghar dining area, this package ensures you're well-fed and utterly relaxed throughout your stay.

Experience

Imagine waking up to misty mornings and birds singing, stepping onto your veranda to soak in the peaceful surroundings. With this package, it's not just about the accommodation—it's about the adventure! Explore the Bhadra Tiger Reserve on exciting jeep and boat safaris, or dive into thrilling activities like kayaking and swimming. When the day winds down, gather around a campfire or enjoy captivating wildlife documentaries. This is more than just a trip; it's an experience to treasure!

Explore Beyond River Tern Lodge

Here are some nearby attractions to River Tern Lodge, along with their approximate distances and travel times

Kemmangundi:

Approximately 54 kilometers from the lodge, Kemmangundi is a scenic hill station once the summer retreat of the Mysore Royals. Depending on road conditions, the drive typically takes 1.5 hours.

Hebbe Falls:

This beautiful waterfall is over 10 kilometers away from Kemmangundi. Reaching Hebbe Falls involves a trek or a jeep ride from Kemmangundi, which can take 2 hours.

Mullayanagiri:

The highest peak in Karnataka, Mullayanagiri, is approximately 12 kilometers from Chikmagalur. The drive from River Tern Lodge to Mullayanagiri can take 2.5 to 3 hours.

Kudremukh:

Approximately 95 kilometers southwest of Chikmagalur town, Kudremukh is known for its unique peak resembling a horse's face and is a popular trekking destination. The journey from River Tern Lodge to Kudremukh can take 2 hours by car.

Sringeri:

A temple town known for its ancient temples and spiritual significance, Sringeri is approximately 90 kilometers from River Tern Lodge. The drive can take around 2 to 2.5 hours.

Belur:

Known for its temples dating back to the Hoysala period, Belur is approximately 104 kilometers from Chikmagalur. The drive from River Tern Lodge to Belur can take 2.5 hours.

Halebeedu:

Once the capital of the Hoysala Empire, Halebeedu is approximately 95 kilometers from Chikmagalur. The journey from River Tern Lodge to Halebeedu can take 2 to 2.5 hours.

Annapoorneshwari Temple in Hornadu:

A famous temple believed to have been established by Adi Shankaracharya, located in Hornadu town. The temple is approximately 122 kilometers from River Tern Lodge, and the drive can take around 3 hours.

Please note that these distances and travel times are approximate and can vary based on the mode of transportation and current road conditions.
