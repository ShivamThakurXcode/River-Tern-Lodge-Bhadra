About Us River Tern Lodge

Welcome to River Tern Lodge

A serene retreat nestled on the picturesque hillocks overlooking the tranquil Bhadra Reservoir in Karnataka's Chikmagalur district. Our lodge, named after the nearby islands that attract hundreds of river terns during their breeding season, offers guests a unique and thrilling opportunity to witness this natural spectacle up close.

A Perfect Blend of Nature and Comfort

For those seeking adventure, River Tern Lodge offers a range of water-based activities on the Bhadra Reservoir, including kayaking, sailing, and joy fishing. These activities are all supervised by trained experts who adhere to stringent safety guidelines, ensuring a secure and enjoyable experience for our guests.

Explore the Wilderness

Situated just 4 kilometres from the Bhadra Tiger Reserve, our lodge is an ideal base for wildlife enthusiasts. Guests can embark on jeep and boat safaris to explore the reserve's rich biodiversity, which includes tigers, leopards, elephants, gaurs, and a variety of bird species, including the Malabar pied hornbill and grey-headed fish eagle.

Adventure and Recreation

For those seeking adventure, River Tern Lodge offers a range of water-based activities on the Bhadra Reservoir, including kayaking, sailing, and joy fishing. These activities are all supervised by trained experts who adhere to stringent safety guidelines, ensuring a secure and enjoyable experience for our guests.

Culinary Delights

Our Gol Ghar, a group dining area, serves a delectable buffet featuring both vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes. Guests can savour local flavours while enjoying panoramic views of the reservoir. The open-air setting and the soothing sounds of nature enhance the dining experience. Meals are crafted to provide a wholesome dining experience, complementing the serene ambience of the lodge.

Plan Your Visit

River Tern Lodge is accessible by road, approximately 283 kilometres from Bengaluru. The nearest railway stations are Tarikere and Shimoga, connecting to major cities, while the closest airport is the Shivamogga domestic airport, around 24 kilometres away. The lodge is a year-round destination, offering a unique experience of the Western Ghats' natural beauty each season.
