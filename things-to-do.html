Things to Do at River Tern Lodge, Bhadra

Situated on the peaceful banks of the Bhadra Reservoir, River Tern Lodge is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike. Whether you're a birdwatcher, a wildlife enthusiast, or someone who enjoys the tranquillity of the great outdoors, a diverse range of activities are waiting for you. Let's delve into the top experiences you can look forward to!.

1. Photography Heaven

You're in for a treat if you've got a camera or even your smartphone. The views here are nothing short of magical. Think misty mornings over calm waters, lush greenery stretching into the horizon, and, if you're lucky, a river tern or two posing just right. It's no wonder photographers keep returningâ€”it's like nature's Instagram feed brought to life.

2. Wildlife Film Shows

Fancy winding down with a wildlife documentary after an adventurous day? Every evening, the lodge hosts film screenings highlighting the rich biodiversity of the Bhadra Tiger Reserve. It's like a mini cinema under the stars, giving you a peek into the lives of the creatures you might have spotted during the day.

3. Thrilling Jeep Safaris

Get adrenaline pumping with a jeep safari through the nearby Bhadra Tiger Reserve. With some luck (and patience), you might spot majestic creatures like leopards, elephants, or even the elusive tiger. The guides know their stuff, so you'll also learn a lot about the local flora and fauna along the way.

4. Boat Safaris on Bhadra Reservoir

Gliding across the still waters of the reservoir on a boat safari feels almost meditativeâ€”until you spot a marsh crocodile lounging nearby or a kingfisher diving for its dinner! The boat ride gives you a whole new perspective of the area, perfectly blending calm and thrill.

5. Kayaking and Swimming

If you love water, you're in the right place. Kayaking lets you explore the reservoir at your own paceâ€”paddle through serenity. Would you prefer a splash? Dive into the designated swimming spots for a refreshing dip. Safety measures are top-notch, so you can let loose and enjoy.

6. Campfires Under the Stars

There's nothing quite like gathering around a roaring campfire after sunset. Swap stories, roast marshmallows, or sit back and stare at the stars. The simple joy stays with you long after you've left.

7. Birdwatching Galore

River Tern Lodge is a birdwatcher's dream. Over 200 species call this area home, including the iconic river tern, grey junglefowl, and Malabar pied hornbill. Keep your binoculars handyâ€”you never know what feathered friend might make an appearance!

8. Suspension Bridge Adventures

While the lodge doesn't boast a suspension bridge, the surrounding area offers plenty of spots to explore. A short trip to the nearby landscapes might lead you to hidden gems perfect for a quick photo op or a peaceful stroll.

More to Explore

Feeling adventurous? Try guided treks through the nearby hills or mountain biking along scenic trails. For a change of pace, visit local attractions like the Tyverekoppa Lion and Tiger Safari or the Sakkrebyle Elephant Camp. And when it's time to refuel, head to the lodge's Gol Ghar dining area for a delicious spread of South and North Indian dishes.

Why Wait?

River Tern Lodge isn't just a place to stayâ€”it's a gateway to adventure, relaxation, and unforgettable memories. So, pack your bags, bring your curiosity, and let nature work its magic. Ready to escape the hustle and bustle? This is your chance to make it happen!
