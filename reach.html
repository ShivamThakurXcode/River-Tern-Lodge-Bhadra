How to Reach River Tern Lodge, Bhadra

Planning a trip to River Tern Lodge? You're in for a treat! Nestled by the serene Bhadra Reservoir, this place is a perfect getaway to reconnect with nature. But first, let's figure out how to get there from Kempegowda International Airport in Bengaluru (IATA: BLR).

Route Description

If you enjoy a scenic drive, hitting the road is the best way to get there. River Tern Lodge is roughly 275 kilometres from the Bangalore airport, and it'll take you about 6–7 hours, depending on traffic and how often you stop to soak in the views

  • Kickstart Your Journey

    Once you're out of the airport, follow the signs for NH 44 (Bellary Road). This highway is your ticket out of the city chaos and into tranquillity.

  • Follow the Trail

    From NH 44, make your way towards Tumkur. You'll bypass the town and continue on a route that takes you through Gubbi, Tiptur, Arsikere, Kadur, and Tarikere. It's a mix of bustling, small, peaceful cities and stretches, so keep your playlist ready!

  • Final Stretch

    After passing Tarikere, look for the turn towards Kuvempu University. From here, Lakkavalli village is just 14 km away. Once you're at Lakkavalli, follow the signs to River Tern Lodge, about 4 km further. Easy

Route Description

Do you prefer train travel? The closest railway stations are:

  • Tarikere (21 km away)
  • Shimoga (Shivamogga) (32 km away)

Trains from Bengaluru connect to both stations, and you can hire a cab for the last leg of your journey.

  • What Makes the Drive Special?

    Picture this: winding roads, lush greenery on either side and the occasional sight of grazing cattle or chirping birds. The journey isn't just about reaching River Tern Lodge; it's an experience. Think of it as an appetizer before the main course!

  • Follow the Trail

    So, are you ready to hit the road or hop on a train? Whichever route you pick, River Tern Lodge awaits with its stunning landscapes and soothing vibes. Trust me, it's worth every kilometre.
