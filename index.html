About us

River Tern Lodge Bhadra

Welcome to River Tern Lodge, nestled in the heart of the Bhadra Wildlife Sanctuary. Our eco-friendly lodge offers a perfect blend of comfort and wilderness experience, situated along the serene backwaters of the Bhadra Reservoir. Named after the River Tern birds that nest here, our lodge provides an authentic wildlife.

Experience the magic of nature with our expert naturalists who guide you through the rich biodiversity of the sanctuary. From tiger sightings to bird watching, every moment at River Tern Lodge is crafted to create unforgettable memories.
